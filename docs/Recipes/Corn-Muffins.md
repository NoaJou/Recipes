### Ingredients
- 2 tbs butter
	- To make honey butter mix 1/4 cup of butter with a tablespoon of honey
- 2 tbs oil
- 1.5 cups cornmeal
- 1 cup flour
- 3.5 tsp baking powder
- pinch of salt
- 2 large eggs
- dollop of mayo
- 2 cups buttermilk/kefir
