> Serves: 2 | Time: 20min
### Ingredients
- 1 cup Basmati rice
- 1 1/2 cup water
- (Optional) 2-4 pods Cardamom
- (Optional) 1 Star Anise
### Steps
1. Wash rice well
2. Bring water to a boil with the rice, cardamom and star anise
3. Turn down to simmer for 7-8min. Donâ€™t open the lid.
4. Fluff up the rice and serve.
